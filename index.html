please don't assume a nickname

Hi 👋 Imagine, in an attempt to be more friendly, you actually end up annoying the person.

Nicknames are amazing! But automatically shortening a name is risky - someone might actually prefer the full version of their name. In an attempt to establish camaraderie, you might be achieving the exact opposite of what you are trying to do. When in doubt, ask! People will happily share their preferred name. It might be their full name, or maybe a non-traditional nickname.
